Nalanda

Incredible India

Nalanda was a renowned Buddhist University in the ancient kingdom of Magadha (modern-day Bihar) in India. Pali was the main language which was used in the old Buddhist text. Buddhist texts describe it as a Mahavihara, a revered Buddhist monastery.The university of Nalanda obtained significant fame, prestige and relevance during ancient times, and rose to legendary status due to its contribution to the emergence of India as a great power around the fourth century.
